How to Use

  1. Click "Open Overlay" to open the broadcast view in a new tab/window
  2. Click "Open Control Panel" to open the control panel in another tab/window
  3. In the control panel, enter team names as they are drawn
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update the overlay in real-time
  5. The overlay will update automatically - no refresh needed!
  6. Use "Clear All" to reset everything for a new draw
